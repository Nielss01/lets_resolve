01 — Wordmark
Logo & Wordmark
The Lets Revolve wordmark uses DM Sans Bold. "Lets" is set in sky blue (#0EA5E9) and "Revolve" in the contextual contrast color. The two-tone split reinforces the brand's dual nature: approachable yet professional.
